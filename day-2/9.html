Swapnil Singh (born 22 January 1991) is an Indian cricketer who plays for Baroda in domestic cricket. He is a bowler who bats right-handed and bowls slow left-arm orthodox. He made his first-class debut for Baroda in 2006 at the age of 14 and was a member of the Mumbai Indians squad in 2008. In a match of the 2014–15 Syed Mushtaq Ali Trophy against Saurashtra, he had bowling figures of 4–0–19–6.
